Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

At StayFinder, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both guests and property owners. The specific cancellation terms may vary by property, so please review the individual property's cancellation policy before booking.

2. Standard Cancellation Policy

For most properties on StayFinder:
Cancellation Time Refund Amount
More than 7 days before check-in Full refund (minus service fee)
3-7 days before check-in 50% refund (minus service fee)
Less than 3 days before check-in No refund
No-show No refund

3. Flexible Cancellation Policy

Some properties offer flexible cancellation terms:

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in
  • Full refund (minus service fee) for cancellations made within the allowed timeframe
  • Look for the "Flexible Cancellation" badge on property listings

4. Strict Cancellation Policy

Some properties have strict cancellation terms:

  • No refund for cancellations
  • Only refundable if the property becomes unavailable
  • Typically offered at discounted rates

5. How to Cancel

To cancel your booking:

  1. Log in to your StayFinder account
  2. Go to "My Bookings" section
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click on "Cancel Booking" and follow the prompts
  5. Confirm the cancellation and review refund details

6. Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Service fees are non-refundable
  • Processing fees may apply depending on your payment method

7. Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure events (natural disasters, government restrictions, pandemics, etc.), we may offer special cancellation terms. These will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis and communicated to affected guests.

4. Property Owner Cancellations

If a property owner needs to cancel your booking:

  • You will receive a full refund including service fees
  • We will assist you in finding alternative accommodation
  • The property owner's rating may be affected

8. No-Show Policy

If you fail to check in by the specified time without prior notice:

  • No refund will be provided
  • The property may release your room to other guests
  • Contact the property directly if you're running late

9. Early Check-out

If you decide to check out early:

  • No refund for unused nights
  • Notify the property management as a courtesy
  • Some properties may offer partial refunds at their discretion

10. Dispute Resolution

If you have any issues with your cancellation or refund:

  1. Contact the property owner first
  2. If unresolved, reach out to StayFinder support
  3. Provide all relevant booking and communication details
  4. We will mediate to find a fair resolution

11. Special Circumstances

For medical emergencies, family emergencies, or other special circumstances:

  • Contact our support team immediately
  • Provide documentation if possible
  • We will review your case on an individual basis
  • Approval is at the discretion of StayFinder and the property owner

12. Contact Information

For questions about our cancellation policy or assistance with cancellations:

  • Email: support@stayfinder.com
  • Phone: +91 1800-123-4567
  • Available 24/7 for urgent cancellation requests
